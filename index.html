About the Workshop

The Fabrics Workshop explores advancements in interconnect technologies such as InfiniBand, Slingshot, and Omni-Path. We aim to foster collaboration between academia and industry for building next-generation high-performance fabrics.

Important Dates

  • Submission Deadline: May 20, 2025
  • Notification of Acceptance: June 10, 2025
  • Workshop Date: July 15, 2025

Call for Participation

We invite researchers, engineers, and practitioners to submit papers, demos, or posters related to HPC fabrics, interconnect topologies, and performance optimizations.

  • Scalable fabric architectures
  • Network congestion control
  • Collective operations over fabrics
  • Performance benchmarking of interconnects
